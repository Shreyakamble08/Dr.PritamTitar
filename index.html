Meet Dr. Pritam Titar

Dr. Pritam Titar is a leading Interventional Cardiologist dedicated to providing compassionate, evidence-based, and accessible cardiac care across Pune. He combines expertise with a patient-centered approach to ensure that every individual receives the attention and care they deserve.
