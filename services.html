Our Medical Services

Comprehensive Healthcare Solutions for Your Well-being

Angioplasty

Angioplasty is a medical procedure used to open narrowed or blocked arteries in the heart.

  • Balloon tube opens blocked artery
  • A stent is placed to keep the artery open.
  • Inflated to push plaque aside
Book Consultation

Cardiac Rehabilitation

Cardiac Rehabilitation is a structured program designed to help individuals recover after heart-related conditions or surgeries.

  • Supervised exercise to strengthen the heart
  • Nutrition and lifestyle guidance
  • Reduces risk of future heart problems
Book Consultation

Angiography

Angiography is a diagnostic procedure used to visualize the inside of blood vessels, especially arteries in the heart.

  • Uses contrast dye and X-rays to view arteries
  • Detects blockages or narrowed vessels
  • Helps plan treatments like angioplasty or surgery
Book Consultation

Balloon Mitral Valvotomy

Balloon Mitral Valvotomy is a minimally invasive treatment for narrowed mitral valves in the heart.

  • Treats narrowed mitral valve without open-heart surgery
  • A balloon is inflated to open the valve
  • Improves blood flow and eases breathlessness.
Book Consultation

Heart Valve Surgery

Heart Valve Surgery is performed to repair or replace one or more damaged heart valves that affect blood flow through the heart.

  • Repairs or replaces faulty heart valves
  • Improves blood circulation and heart efficiency
  • Can be done through open or minimally invasive surgery
Book Consultation

Bypass Surgery

Bypass Surgery is a procedure to restore blood flow to the heart by creating a new path around blocked arteries.

  • Uses a blood vessel graft from leg, arm, or chest.
  • Improves blood supply to heart muscle.
  • Reduces risk of heart attack and relieves chest pain.
Book Consultation

Septal Defect Closure

Septal Defect Closure is a treatment to fix a hole in the heart between its chambers for better blood flow.

  • Closes holes between heart chambers (ASD/VSD).
  • Can be done surgically or via a catheter-based device.
  • Prevents heart failure, irregular heartbeat, and blood mixing.
Book Consultation

Varicose Vein Treatment

Varicose Vein Treatment involves procedures to relieve pain and improve appearance by closing or removing enlarged, twisted veins.

  • Minimally invasive methods like laser therapy or sclerotherapy are commonly used.
  • Improves blood circulation and reduces pain or swelling.
  • Enhances cosmetic appearance by removing bulging veins.
Book Consultation

Pacemaker

A pacemaker is a small device placed in the chest to help control abnormal heart rhythms.

  • Regulates slow or irregular heartbeats.
  • Implanted under the skin near the chest.
  • Sends electrical signals to maintain heart rhythm.
Book Consultation
