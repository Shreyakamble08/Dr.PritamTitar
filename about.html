MEET YOUR DOCTOR

Dr. Pritam R. Titar, MD
Cardiologist & Internist

Dr. Pritam R. Titar, M.B.B.S., M.D. (Medicine), DrNB (Cardiology), is an experienced interventional and consultant cardiologist based in Pune. He completed his medical education at B.J. Medical College, Pune, followed by an M.D. in Medicine from Topiwala National Medical College & BYL Nair Hospital, Mumbai.

His interventional specialties include coronary and peripheral angioplasty, pacemaker implantation, device closures (ASD, VSD, PDA), balloon mitral valvotomy, and advanced techniques like rotablation, CTO, and IVUS/OCT.
