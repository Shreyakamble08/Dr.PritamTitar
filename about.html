Dr. Pritam Titar
Interventional Cardiologist

Dr. Pritam Titar is an interventional cardiologist dedicated to delivering advanced, ethical, and patient-focused cardiac care across Pune district. With a passion for preventive cardiology and early detection of heart disease, he combines modern diagnostic tools with evidence-based treatment to provide comprehensive care for patients of all age groups.

He practices across multiple hospitals to ensure accessible heart care, especially in fast-growing suburbs and semi-urban regions where timely cardiology services are crucial.

"Every patient deserves personalized, timely, and compassionate cardiac care."
